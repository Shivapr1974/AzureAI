# AI Adoption and Human-Centered Design

## Learning Objectives
- Describe key motivators and impediments to enterprise AI adoption.
- Articulate principles of human-centered design in the context of AI.

## Key Motivators for AI Adoption
- **Value Proposition**: AI enables organizations to capture significant value.
- **Problem Solving**: AI can solve previously unsolvable problems using traditional analytics.
- **Team Benefits**: All teams can benefit from AI solutions.
- **Quantitative ROI**: Organizations can realize true, quantitative returns on AI investments.

## Barriers to AI Implementation
- **Measurement Challenges**: 19% find it hard to measure AI's value.
- **Understanding AI**: 19% lack understanding of AI benefits.
- **Data Issues**: 15% face data accessibility challenges.
- **Quality Problems**: 9% report data scope or quality issues.
- **Skills Gap**: 6% lack necessary staff skills.
- **Technology Knowledge**: 5% lack knowledge of technology.

## Booz Allen's AI Adoption Blueprint
1. **AI Operations Integration**: Integrate DataOps, DevSecOps, and MLOps.
2. **Analytic Frameworks**: Implement advanced analytics and machine learning frameworks.
3. **Digital Architecture**: Establish modular reference architecture for integration.
4. **Data Engineering**: Develop an enterprise data strategy through DataOps.
5. **Responsible AI**: Incorporate risk evaluation for responsible AI adoption.

## Objectives for AIOps Deployment
- Set a clear vision for AI capability.
- Articulate goals tied to organizational outcomes.
- Identify AI culture champions.
- Establish short-term projects to demonstrate value.

## Indicators for AI Project Success
- **Data Readiness**: Accessible, high-quality datasets.
- **Infrastructure Readiness**: Flexible and secure environments.
- **Organizational Readiness**: Transparent and well-led organization.
- **Analytic Techniques**: Backed by literature and experience.
- **Leadership Buy-In**: Executive sponsorship and end-user adoption.
- **Defined Business Problem**: Clear scope and ROI.
- **Team Expertise**: Diverse team with tech and non-tech skills.
- **Project Cohesion**: Committed project team.
- **Agile Management**: Iterative, feedback-centric approach.

## What is Human-Centered AI?
- **Definition**: Combines ethics and human-computer interactions, ensuring humans control technology.
- **Goals**:
  - Develop AI with humanity in mind.
  - Advance trustworthy AI congruent with societal values.
  - Understand AI's societal impact.
  - Protect human rights.

## Critical Themes for AI Solutions Success
1. **Human + AI Collaboration**: Better outcomes through collaboration.
2. **Responsible AI**: Fair, unbiased, and ethical AI use.
3. **Communication**: Engaging conversational user interfaces.

- You should now be able to describe motivators and impediments to AI adoption and articulate human-centered design principles in AI.
